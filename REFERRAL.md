| Rule | Detail |

| **Fork required** | Your GitHub account must have the ACO repo forked |
| **Star required** | Your GitHub account must have starred the ACO repo |

## FAQ

**Q: Can I refer developers who already use Goofre?**
Yes — as long as they don't already have a fork, their new fork counts.

**Q: Does it work if my 3 referrals sign up but don't fork?**
No. The fork is the qualifying action, not the sign-up.

**Q: When does the 6-month clock start?**
It starts the day your coupon code is applied to a Goofre OS account — not at claim time.

**Q: What happens after 6 months?**
Your account continues on the standard plan. You'll receive a reminder before the free period ends.

**Q: Can my referrals also refer others and get the reward?**
Yes — every developer who forks can generate their own referral link and participate in the same program.
